About the Team

Everyone that contributed to this project is a student and/or employee at Lambda School. The Spotify Song Suggester is a project developed for Lambda's Build Week. Our team is a mix of students in the Web Development and Data Science tracks. Below is a gallery of each team member that describes everyone's role in building the Spotify Song Suggester.

Nayan Doshi

Nayan Doshi is both a student and a mentor at Lambda School. For this build week, Nayan acted as the project manager of this team. As PL, his responsibilities include remaining available for any team members who might encounter issues; checking in everyday with the team to determine whether everyone’s on track; and generally just manage all the chaos of git-flow and collaborative coding!
